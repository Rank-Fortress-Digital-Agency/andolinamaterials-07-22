What is mulch and why should you use it in your landscaping project


Mulch is a layer of material (usually organic) that is spread over the surface of soil. It has many benefits, including:

  • suppressing weeds
  • reducing evaporation
  • improving soil fertility
  • providing a clean, finished look to your landscaping project.

In general, mulch is an effective and low-maintenance way to improve the health and appearance of your landscaping. Andolina Materials is proud to offer high-quality mulch sourced from local suppliers.

How to install mulch in your landscape

Mulch is a material, usually organic, that is spread over the surface of the soil. It serves many purposes in landscaping, including weed control, moisture retention, and temperature regulation. Mulch also helps to prevent erosion and can add aesthetic appeal to your landscape. There are many different types of mulch available on the market, so it’s important to choose one that is right for your needs. Here are some tips on how to install mulch in your landscape:

  • Choose the right type of mulch for your needs. There are many different types of mulch available, such as bark mulch, wood chips, leaves, and straw. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, so it’s important to choose one that is right for your needs.
  • Prepare the area where you will be installing the mulch. Remove any existing vegetation, such as weeds, and loosen the soil with a shovel or rake.
  • Apply the mulch to the prepared area. Spread it evenly over the surface of the soil, using a rake or your hands.
  • Water the area thoroughly after applying the mulch. This will help it to settle into the soil and start doing its job.
